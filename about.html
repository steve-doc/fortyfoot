Safety

There are many safety warning signs at the 40 Foot. Please take care to read them before entering the water. The main hazards are the rocks, both visible and beneath the water, so pay particular attention when entering.. Higher tides are the preferred time to go swimming. Please also pay attention to the weather forecast to avoid high waves. It is always recommended you swim with another competent swimmer.

How to get here

  • Bicycle - Use the coastal mobility route where possible. Plenty of cycle parking. Also possible to use public bike rental apps.
  • DART - Sandycove and Glastule station - 10 min walk.
  • Bus - Take 59 bus and get off at stop 3052 on Elton Park - 5 min walk.
  • Car - Parking is extremely limited and confined to street parking.
